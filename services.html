Patch Types We Offer

Embroidered Patches

Traditional patches with raised thread designs, offering a classic and textured look.

Woven Patches

Flat patches with intricate details, perfect for designs with small text or fine lines.

PVC Patches

Durable, waterproof patches with a 3D effect, ideal for outdoor and tactical use.

Chenille Patches

Fuzzy, textured patches commonly used for varsity letters and sports emblems.

Printed Patches

Full-color patches with photographic quality, perfect for complex designs.

Leather Patches

Premium patches made from genuine or synthetic leather for a sophisticated look.

Backing Options

Iron-On

Heat-activated adhesive backing for easy application to fabric.

Sew-On

Traditional backing for permanent attachment to garments.

Hook & Loop (Velcro)

Removable backing ideal for tactical gear and uniforms.

Self-Adhesive

Peel-and-stick backing for non-fabric surfaces.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity is 25 patches. For smaller quantities, please contact us for custom pricing.

How long does production take?

Standard production time is 2-3 weeks after design approval. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee.

Can you match specific colors?

Yes, we can match specific Pantone colors for your patches to ensure brand consistency.

Do you offer design services?

Yes, our in-house design team can create custom patch designs based on your ideas or refine existing artwork.

What file formats do you accept for designs?

We accept vector files (AI, EPS, PDF) for best results. We can also work with high-resolution JPG or PNG files.
