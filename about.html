About Patch Dispatcher

Crafting quality patches since 2010

Our Story

Patch Dispatcher began with a simple mission: to create high-quality, custom patches that tell stories and represent identities. Founded in 2010 by a group of textile enthusiasts, our company has grown from a small workshop to a leading provider of premium patches.

What started as a passion project has evolved into a business that serves clients worldwide, from military units and corporate brands to fashion designers and sports teams. Our dedication to craftsmanship and attention to detail has earned us a reputation for excellence in the industry.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our capabilities, always staying true to our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
